Welcome to Interesting Capybara

Meet Safetybara, the capybara who always wears his PPE, checks his forklift before use, and follows every sign in the warehouse. He’s chill, he’s competent, and most importantly, he’s OSHA compliant.

We make funny and functional stickers and posters for manufacturing workers, warehouse warriors, and anyone who thinks safety can use a little more personality. From "lockout tagout" reminders to "don’t skip ear protection" stickers, Safetybara’s got your back.
